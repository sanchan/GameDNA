FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GameDNA free to use?

Yes! GameDNA is completely free. We believe everyone should have access to personalized game recommendations. No hidden fees, no premium tiers, just pure gaming discovery.

How does the AI recommendation system work?

Our AI analyzes your Steam library, playtime patterns, genre preferences, and gaming behavior across 20+ dimensions. It compares this data with millions of other gamers and game attributes to find your perfect matches with industry-leading accuracy.

Is my Steam data secure?

Absolutely. We use Steam's official OAuth authentication and never store your password. Your data is encrypted, never sold to third parties, and you can delete your account anytime. Privacy is our top priority.

Can I use GameDNA without Steam?

Currently, GameDNA requires a Steam account for the best experience. However, we're working on supporting other platforms like Epic Games, GOG, and Xbox Game Pass in future updates.

How accurate are the match percentages?

Our AI has a 98% satisfaction rate based on user feedback. The more you use GameDNA and swipe on games, the better it understands your preferences and improves match accuracy over time.

Can I share my Gaming DNA with friends?

Yes! You can share your radar chart and gaming profile with friends. Compare your gaming DNA, see compatibility scores, and discover games you both might enjoy. Social features are coming soon!
